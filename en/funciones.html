/// Features

> System > Nutrition > Training > Progress

1. "Zero Cloud" Architecture (100% Offline-First)

No bridge servers, no cloud bullshit, no monthly subscriptions to pay for our AWS bill. The whole system runs natively on your device over IndexedDB. If you lose connection in the middle of the gym or in some sweaty basement, the app doesn't even flinch: it keeps working at 100%.

2. Paranoid Military-Grade Cryptography

We protect your data like it's gold. We use native AES-GCM 256-bit encryption (Web Crypto API). Your PIN goes through 100,000 mathematical iterations (PBKDF2) mixed with a unique salt to forge a master key. That key only exists in RAM and self-destructs upon exit. Plus, we use an 'Auth Canary' system to blind-validate your PIN without exposing a single byte of real data. If you forget your PIN, kiss your data goodbye. We're not your babysitters.

3. Trash-Free "Vanilla" Stack

No React, no Angular, no toxic dependencies weighing hundreds of megabytes. The app is built with pure, raw code—no frameworks, structured in Native Web Components and Shadow DOM. Instant load times, peak performance, and your battery stays intact. The hard way, but the only right way.

4. Universal Native Installation (PWA)

Whether Tim Cook likes it or not, Aerko_ doesn't need middlemen. It’s designed from the ground up to be installed natively on your phone (iOS or Android) straight from the browser. Full screen, no ugly search bars, 100% yours.

5. Anti "Vendor-Lock" Granular Export

If you decide to leave (which I highly doubt), you take your clean data in .json format. If security is enabled, the system decrypts the payload directly into RAM solely and exclusively to generate the file, keeping your local database 100% bulletproof the whole time. We don't hold anyone's data hostage here; if an app traps you with vendor-lock, you should burn it to the ground.

6. Interactive Personalities

An app that talks to you like a generic corporate bot? Boring. Aerko_ packs three integrated 'personalities': Default mode (classic, dry, and direct), Zen mode (gentle and pressure-free, perfect if you're dodging an ED), and the crown jewel: Tsundere mode. This one offers maximum hostility, dark humor, and constant humiliation every time you screw up or skip a workout. If you get offended, that's your own damn problem.

7. Universal Platform Migration

Coming from Hevy, Strong, Lyfta, Apple Health, Google Fit, or Samsung Health? Get in here. We've coded native parsers designed to extract, purify, and inject years of your training history and biometrics straight into Aerko_ without dropping a single lift. The system integrates everything on the fly from your .zip files.

8. Multilingual Support with Smart Fallback

The app is native in Spanish and translated into English, Portuguese, German, and French. Our language engine features dynamic loading and a bulletproof fallback: if you try to use a language that doesn't support Tsundere or Zen mode, the system automatically loads the Default mode texts while keeping full functionality. Pure efficiency.

1. Scientific Wizard & "God Mode"

Forget those 'magical' internet calculators. We use the original Harris-Benedict (1984) formula for your BMR and scientific ISSN ratios to nail your macros. The system applies a transversal reactive recalculation if your weight shifts. And if you think you're smarter than the algorithm, activate 'God Mode' and punch in your data manually.

2. Native Scanner & Hybrid Database

No more searching for food manually like a peasant. We integrated a native barcode scanner that attacks the Open Food Facts API straight from your client. Zero bridge servers, zero telemetry. Plus, you get a meticulously curated hybrid local database so you aren't a slave to your internet connection.

3. Modular Nutrition: Fixed & Variable Grams

Manage food groups and design your base diets. You can assign fixed weights for what you eat identically every single day, or variable weights for what you decide on the spot. Put the effort in once to set it up, and forget about it for the rest of the month.

4. Smart Checks & Adaptive Visual Dashboard

Our Smart Checks flow gives you a zero-friction daily log: you log in, check off your pre-configured meals, and you're done. All your caloric progress renders on an adaptive visual dashboard that tells you exactly how much margin you have left in real-time.

1. Modular Planner & Smart Flow

Design your routine once. At the gym, the Smart Flow engine takes over: you execute your routine with zero friction, log data on the fly, and use native timers without leaving the app. You just lift the heavy circles; we handle the bureaucracy.

2. Predictive Load Engine & Multi-Conditional 1RM

The app analyzes your fatigue patterns and mesocycles to tell you exactly what weight to move in your next session. We dynamically deploy Brzycki, Epley, or Lombardi formulas depending on your rep ranges. Pure math applied to iron.

3. Scientific Volume Analysis & Imbalances

We cross-reference your effective sets with an anatomical database to map your effort into real thresholds: MV, MEV, MAV, and MRV. The system automatically detects imbalances by muscle heads. If you skip leg day, we are going to rub it right in your face.

4. Vision Lab: AI Biomechanics

Record your lifts and our AI will roast your technique, measuring ROM, bar path, and sticking points. We use asynchronous 'Ping-Pong' optimization with dynamic FPS and EMA mathematical filters to protect your CPU and battery while achieving millimeter precision.

1. Anti-Obsession Cooldown & Clinical Biometrics

We implemented a dynamic logging cooldown (tied to your active personality) to protect your mental health and filter out garbage metrics. Log your weight, perimeter measurements, and precise skinfolds only when it actually makes sense.

2. Scientific Body Composition Calculator

Throw your 'smart' scale in the trash. If you want to know your real body fat, use a caliper. Our math engine uses Jackson-Pollock formulas combined with Siri's equation to extract your true body density. Zero magical estimations.

3. Pure Client-Side Dashboards (Zero Telemetry)

Heatmaps, muscle volume radars, e1RM curves... Everything is generated via <canvas> purely on your device. No calls to external servers, no data tracking. Your progress is yours and nobody else's.

4. Aerko_ Wrapped: The Anti-Wrapped

Once every three months, the system renders a visual poster with your strength milestones (and a few insults if you run Tsundere mode) that you can download without wasting data. Share it if you want, then get back to training. Just the right amount of flexing.
