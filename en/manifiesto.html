AERKO_PROTOCOL

This isn't a startup: no VC suits, no marketing fluff, and hell no, I have zero intention of selling your data to buy myself a yacht.

This is an open-code project, but more than that, it's my declaration of war against mediocre software and the greedy corporations that exploit users to line their pockets at the expense of your privacy.

I'm a UX/UI designer and WebDev, but mostly, I'm just someone sick of apps like MyFitnessPal, Hevy, or any other trash whose business model is harvesting your data, holding it hostage, trying to squeeze a subscription out of you, just to end up selling your data anyway.

Yeah, sure, Notepad and Excel "work", but they're a pain in the ass. That's exactly why Aerko was born. I don't just want to offer everyone something "better" than those apps; I'm handing you a tool that is free, private, and actually powerful.

My philosophy is painfully simple: ship something lightweight, genuinely good, and built for humans, not for milking their bank accounts.

That's why everything, from the neo-brutalist design to the vanilla code (built without bloated frameworks), is rigged to give you, the end-user, absolute control. You're welcome.

PRIVACY

I don't care when you read this, my stance won't change. Your data is your data, and honestly? I couldn't care less about it.

I will always give you the tools to export, import, protect (encrypt), and completely nuke your data from existence.

MY_GOAL

My main goal with this app is to drop it on the world, not to get filthy rich (kind of hard when it's free), but to dethrone the current garbage apps and start setting actual standards—both ethical and technological.

And no, I'm not going to sell out or compromise my principles, or the core principles of this app, to make it happen.
